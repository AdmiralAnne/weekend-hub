1. The "Ghost User" System

This application respects your privacy by design. We do not require accounts, passwords, or emails. Instead, we generate a random, anonymous "Ghost ID" stored locally in your browser to remember which mixtapes you created and liked. We do not track personal identities.

2. YouTube API Services

Our mixtape player is powered by the official YouTube IFrame API. By using this application, you are simultaneously interacting with YouTube. We do not host or pirate any audio; all playback is streamed directly from YouTube's servers, adhering to their embedding policies.

3. Community Archive & Moderation

Mixtapes saved to the cloud are publicly visible in the Community Archive. While you have the ability to delete tapes you own, we reserve the right to remove any mixtapes containing offensive titles or links that violate general community guidelines.

4. Local Data (Cookies & Storage)

We use your browser's localStorage purely for functional reasons (saving your current to-do list, your selected weather city, and your Ghost ID). Clearing your browser data will result in losing access to edit your previously burned tapes.
